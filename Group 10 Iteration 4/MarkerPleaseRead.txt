For the color vision deficiency, please note that the game is completely playable without 
needing to see any color. Tiles have symbols representing their terrain type, players
have names and icons, and there are several color alternatives in the settings (royal,
greyscale, and candy).

Here is the link to the .jar on google drive, as it was too big to hold here: https://drive.google.com/file/d/1RMNrPt2B-AOIVSqwiJbxk7Z2viyUNLOv/view?usp=sharing

All source code can be found in ProjectCode/src and ProjectCode/src/Graphics in this repository.
The easiest way to compile it would be to dump those two directories, as well as the resources in the ProjectCode/Images directory, together and then compile
